Gamebonder is a web application that enables you to share your perspective on games by bonding two games together with a unique reason or description that you provide.

As the content grows on the site, we will all be able to have a better view of how games are connected. The purpose of this is to provide a unique perspective to gamers to help them on their search for more games that appeal to their interests.

This is a work in progress so please come back often to see the updates!
